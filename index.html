About Us

What is DevCom ?

Developers Community aka DevCom is a club of Central Institute of Technology Kokrajhar,Assam focuses on the field of Web and App Development among the interested

We conduct events related to the field and focuses on activities such as Web/App Development, Competitions, Seminars, Industry Talks, etc. The Club aims to contribute to the society on Web/App Development from the members with their skills and talent.

  • Web Developers
  • UI/UX Designers
